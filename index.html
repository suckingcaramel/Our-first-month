Our first month

You are my hedgehog

you're as carefree as that hedgehog riding off into the distance and enjoying a tailwind.

You are my seal

Your eyes are as charming as a seal's, they're like a mirror to my soul, more dear to me than anything else in the world.

You are my kitten

Sometimes you get angry like that kitten, but that makes you even cuter in my eyes.

You are my gosling

and at the end of the day, you're still a sweet little goose who's willing to stand up for us and our love.

~You made me believe in love~
